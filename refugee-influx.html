    Refugee Influx

    In collaboration with UNHCR and with funding from DfID, Geo Gecko built a portal, ugandarefugees.org and a series of maps to coordinate the refugee response currently underway in Uganda.

    This map is the second in the series and shows the movement of the refugees into the country.
