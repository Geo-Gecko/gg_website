    GG on lockdown

    Up to 4 weeks ago, 'working from home' meant holing myself up in a nice cafe with a coffee and my laptop. Stark surprise when the corona crisis landed me in home-isolation with my elderly mum ("I can't eat these vegetables"), my 3-year old ("Come mama, come, come see this!" "What?" "Cooooooome maaaaaamaaaaaaaaa") and a dog who insists on nervously prancing around me as I type. All of a sudden the office seems like a mythical oasis of calm.. Will we ever be allowed back in, please?
