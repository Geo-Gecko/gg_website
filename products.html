    Data Insight Portal

    Project Description

    Geo Gecko worked with the Ugandan Bureau of Statistics to process census data and make it available to the public in a searchable and easily digestible format on district level, enter 'DIP Uganda!' We have the ability to expand this portal with further data, even incorporating client's own data, turning the portal into a prime tool to plan commercial and development activities down to sub-county level.

    Project Details

    Client:hgyuguf

    Date: 26th April, 2014

    Tags: Photography, Design

    Visit the portal: DIP Uganda
