    About

    We Create Awesome Stuff

    We monitor how the earth changes over time. We quantify the change that is relevant to your work and provide you with the analysis you need to make an informed decision. We do this by tapping into satellites with almost daily global coverage, use powerful cloud computing tools and develop machine learning algorithms to extract the relevant data. We know the satellites, they know the reality on the ground.

    Crop Health

    Satellite surveillance provides timely and detailed information to manage crop production, such as plant health, soil moisture, rainfall and ground temperature. Our system provides commercial farmers with data related to the health of their crop and associated information twice a month.

    Quantifying Crops And Yeilds

    Through a sensor, each plant has a unique spectral fingerprint and once this fingerprint is identified, large areas can be scanned using machine learning to search for the same signature. This allows us to quantify the square kilometres of a specific crop in a specific area and estimate its yield.

    Forest Identification

    Deforestation is devastating in the African continent due to weak regulations and minimal monitoring. With satellite surveillance, a powerful source of almost daily updates. We typically use this analysis to quantify tree cover within a limited area and analyze it further, e.g. SQ KM of forest cover, forest type etc. and how they change over time, e.g. loss due to fire or harvesting.
